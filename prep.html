Meet the instructors

Andrea Rodriguez

Andrea Rodriguez is not your typical software engineer. While she excels in the world of technology and programming, she has a passion that extends far beyond her day job. Andrea is a dedicated English teacher, who finds joy in helping immigrants learn and master the English language. Her unique blend of technical expertise and a warm, nurturing teaching style make her an exceptional educator. Join Andrea on a journey of language learning and cultural exploration, as she imparts her knowledge and love for the English language to those seeking to find their place in a new land.

Jonathan Robinson

Jonathan Robinson, a dedicated and compassionate GED preparation teacher who is committed to empowering individuals to overcome the barriers that prevented them from completing their high school education. Jonathan's personal journey, having earned his own GED after facing his unique challenges, has inspired him to help others reach their educational goals. Jonathan Robinson is not just a teacher; he's a mentor and a source of inspiration for those seeking a second chance at education.
